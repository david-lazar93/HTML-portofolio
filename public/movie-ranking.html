Top 3 movies to see in 2024

David's top 3 movies in 2024:

**Dune: Part Two**

Denis Villeneuve's epic sequel continues the saga of Paul Atreides as he navigates destiny and vengeance amidst the political and spiritual turmoil of Arrakis. With stunning visuals and an emotional depth that surpasses its predecessor, this film has been lauded as a sci-fi masterpiece.

**The Holdovers**

Directed by Alexander Payne, this heartfelt drama-comedy stars Paul Giamatti as a grumpy teacher supervising students over the Christmas break. Packed with emotional depth and witty dialogue, it explores themes of connection and personal growth.

**Anora**

Sean Baker's Palme d'Or-winning film is a vibrant exploration of love and ambition. It follows a sex worker who marries into the life of a Russian oligarch's son, blending romance, comedy, and drama in an emotionally gripping narrative.
